## Admission Atlas — Armenia

Admission Atlas is an interactive data visualization platform that structures and visualizes bachelor admission data from Armenian universities.

The platform aggregates data from more than 20 official sources and presents them through interactive visualizations to help applicants better understand the admission process and make data-driven decisions.

## Data Sources

The dataset was compiled from more than 20 official sources, including:

- Armenian university official websites
- Admission exam database (dimord.am)
- Ministry of Education admission regulations
- Historical admission score publications
- University faculty pages

The data was manually collected, structured, and normalized to build a unified dataset of Armenian bachelor admission programs.
